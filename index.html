Press / Reviews

“...made Tchaikovsky’s Violin Concerto sound freshly minted, garnering the loudest cheers from the audience.” — Pianofortephilia, 2025

Featured coverage: The Straits Times (National Piano & Violin Competition Final Round).

Ernest Syu is a versatile concert violinist whose artistic presence spans solo, chamber, and orchestral stages. His musicianship is defined by expressive depth, stylistic range, and a commitment to both individual voice and collaborative artistry.

He is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Music in Violin Performance at the Yong Siew Toh Conservatory of Music, National University of Singapore, where he studies with Professor Qian Zhou. Ernest regularly freelances with the Singapore Symphony Orchestra and has served in leadership roles in conservatory and chamber ensembles.

He performs on a 1726 Matteo Goffriller violin, generously loaned by the Rin Collection in Singapore.
