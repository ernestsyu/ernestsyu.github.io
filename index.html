Ernest (Cheng-Yi) Syu

A dynamic young artist shaping his voice across concerto, chamber, and orchestral platforms.

Biography

Taiwanese violinist Ernest (Cheng-Yi) Syu is an active performer based in Singapore. He is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Music in Violin Performance at the Yong Siew Toh Conservatory of Music, National University of Singapore, under the tutelage of Professor Qian Zhou. He regularly freelances with the Singapore Symphony Orchestra and serves as rotating concertmaster of the Yong Siew Toh Conservatory Orchestra. He has appeared as soloist in Tchaikovsky’s Violin Concerto and other major works, and was awarded First Prize in the 2025 National Piano & Violin Competition (Artist Category).
