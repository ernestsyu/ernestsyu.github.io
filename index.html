Ernest Syu

Violinist

About

Ernest (Cheng-Yi) Syu is a violinist currently studying at the Yong Siew Toh Conservatory of Music, National University of Singapore, under Professor Qian Zhou. He regularly freelances with the Singapore Symphony Orchestra.

Ernest is the First Prize winner of the 2025 Singapore National Piano and Violin Competition (Artist Category) and winner of the YST Conservatory Concerto Competition. He has performed Tchaikovsky Violin Concerto with the YST Conservatory Orchestra and has appeared internationally in festivals across Europe and Asia.
